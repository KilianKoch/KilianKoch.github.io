Curriculum Vitae

I am a mathematician and doctoral researcher at the Chair of Applied Analysis at RWTH Aachen University. My research concerns gradient flows in geometric analysis, in particular well-posedness and singular behaviour of half-harmonic map heat flows, for instance due to topological constraints. My position is funded by the DFG through CRC 1481, project C01.


Alongside my research I build practical software, for example business workflow automation; see Software.
