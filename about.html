About Me

Welcome! I'm a mathematician and software developer with a passion for solving complex problems. My journey began with a fascination for numbers and logical reasoning, which naturally led me to pursue mathematics. Along the way, I discovered the power of programming as a tool to model and solve real-world problems.

My research interests include algebraic topology, numerical analysis, and computational mathematics. In the realm of software development, I specialize in developing efficient algorithms and creating scalable applications.

When I'm not crunching numbers or coding, I enjoy hiking, playing chess, and exploring the latest advancements in technology.
