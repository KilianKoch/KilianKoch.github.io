About Me

Hello, I'm Kilian Koch, a mathematician and software developer. I have a master's degree in mathematics with a minor in Computer Science Applications. My main focus is on Applied Analysis and Mathematical Physics.


In mathematics I work on gradient flow analysis, in particular on well-posedness and singular behaviour, e.g. due to topological constraints, such as the harmonic map heat flow. In software, I develop practical solutions to real-world problems, such as business workflow automation and customer data management.

Through my experience of working directly with customers at various events and managing information, I have developed strong customer communication skills. The combination of maths and software development gives me excellent problem solving skills.


Outside of work, I enjoy calisthenics, weight training, and boxing.
