KoKi

A comprehensive management tool, continuously developed since 2019, designed to enhance productivity and automate workflows. KoKi provides an intuitive solution for managing customer information alongside their vehicles, with advanced features tailored for the needs of Auto-Koch GmbH & Co. KG, where I am currently employed.

Features

  • Customer and vehicle Management
  • Detailed vehicle history and customer information management
  • Automated scheduling for inspections and TÜV (general and emissions inspections) based on custom algorithms developed in collaboration with Auto-Koch
  • Complex search functionalities for warranties, overdue calls, etc.
  • Automatic PDF generation for postal communication
  • SMS and email integration via APIs for automatic appointment confirmations
  • Chat for easy communication between employees, including sharing vehicle details
  • Custom specifications for better data handling and user experience

This project is developed in close cooperation with the users at Auto-Koch GmbH & Co. KG, where I work, and is continuously evolving with new features like automatic appointment reminders.
